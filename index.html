About VIT Campus

Inspired by the thoughts and ideas as propounded by Swami Vivekananda and learning from the experiences of successfully running Engineering Colleges . The Promoters decided to make a steer beginning in the direction of man-making engineering education and launched Institutes namely VIVEKANANDA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY and VIVEKANANDA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY(EAST). These Institutes develop physical, mental, emotional and spiritual faculties of students with a view to make them techno-managers of global standard.
The sprawling and lush green campus is spread over 45 acres of land and is surrounded by JDA approved residential colonies.VIT Campus Jaipur is a pioneer in the field of technical education and is about 20 km from Jaipur Bus Stand (Sindhi Camp) and the Jaipur Railway station. The campus can also be accessed by the nearest railway station at Jagatpura falling only 5 kms away.

Overview of workshop

Android App Development Workshop mainly focuses on how to use Android OS for building your own Android Application.The workshop will start from the basics like designing layouts and building complex layouts. Once the basics of Android are done we will begin with building Apps.
Web Designing is the process of creating websites. It encompasses several different aspects, including webpage layout, content production, and graphic design.
The duration of this workshop will be two consecutive days, divided into theory and hand's-on practical sessions. At the end of this workshop, a competition will be organized among the participating students where Winners will get Certificate of Merit while each participants will get Certificate of Participation.
